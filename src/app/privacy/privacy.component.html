SMARTN8N (“the App”) is a Gmail-connected automation tool developed by SMARTSOFT. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the App.

By using SMARTN8N, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please discontinue using the App.

3. Google API Limited Use Requirements

SMARTN8N complies with the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

This means:

  • We only use data to provide and improve the core features of the App.
  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not use your Gmail data for advertising.
  • We do not transfer Gmail data to external services except as necessary to operate the App.
  • We only access, process, and store Gmail data as required to provide the service you configured.

4. Information We Collect

4.1 Gmail Data

SMARTN8N accesses Gmail data only with your explicit OAuth consent. Depending on your configuration, the App may:

  • Read your emails
  • Send emails on your behalf

We do not read or access your entire inbox indiscriminately. We only access emails necessary for the automation and workflows you configure.

4.2 Automatically Collected Data

To operate the service, we may collect and log:

  • Email metadata (message ID, sender, recipient, date, labels).
  • Logs of automation actions performed (emails read, emails sent).
  • Error logs and diagnostic information.

We do not store full email content unless explicitly required by an automation you configure.

5. How We Use Your Information

Your Gmail data is used exclusively for:

  • Reading incoming emails and extracting necessary information.
  • Sending automated or templated emails you configure.
  • Triggering workflows based on your n8n automation rules.
  • Improving performance, reliability, and support.
  • Debugging, security monitoring, and abuse prevention.

We do not use Gmail data for advertising, analytics, profiling, or marketing.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We will never share your Gmail data with:

  • Advertisers
  • Marketing tools
  • Data brokers

Data is shared only if required for:

  • Essential service providers (hosting, infrastructure).
  • Legal compliance, court orders, or law enforcement requests.

7. Data Storage and Retention

We retain information only for as long as needed to operate the App.

  • Metadata and logs may be stored temporarily for debugging and security.
  • Email bodies are not stored unless your automation requires it.
  • You may request full deletion of your data at any time.

8. Data Security

We apply strong security practices, including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all communication.
  • Secure servers and restricted access.
  • OAuth 2.0 authentication.
  • Infrastructure monitoring and security best practices.

While we strive to protect your data, no system can be 100% secure.
